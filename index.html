Welcome to Sultanı MUN 2021!

The fifth session of GSLMUN, held on December 2019, was a great success with over 750 participants from all over the world. GSLMUN, with its highly motivated organizer board and international student officer and press team, ensures a smooth process before, during and after the conference through innovative practices such as the online approval panel and online participant system, which in accordance with our green policy helps minimize paper waste. We are certain that GSLMUN will continue to maintain its high quality of debate and the friendly atmosphere among its international participants in the upcoming year. Our aim is to gather enthusiastic participants and provide them a chance to make connections from around the world, enjoy a magnificent city, and engage in quality debates at the same time. We hope to achieve this goal by blending our own experience as a MUN club with your participation, and as a result, creating a productive and enjoyable environment in a peaceful atmosphere. Our conference abides by the HMUN rules of procedure within its committees. GSLMUN has been and will be held in Galatasaray High School,The second oldest educational institution inTurkey. Our school,with its 153 year old campus which is one of the signature landmarks of Istanbul will guarantee that our visitors will have an unforgettable experience.
